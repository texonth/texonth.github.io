About Me

Nick Mantis

  • DJ, musician and producer.
  • Lives and works in St. Petersburg.
  • The main directions in music: House, Trap, Dubstep, Ambient.

In 2015 he publishes his first collection of tracks «MONOSCRIPT». In the same year he entered the top 100 in the directions PromoDJ Trap, Twerk. February 8, 2016 signed a contract with the American label «Media Blackout», then the light goes out in the release Deep Chill Unwind & Rewind, where Mantis standing on a par with such well-known headliners like Le Flex, M.Waxx. In the fall of 2016 came a remix of Robbie Williams song "Party Like A Russian", which was later published in the French edition of "Robbie Williams Live". In March 2017 release announced on Leble "Stazis" (Proton) and "After Mess Recs" - May 2017.
